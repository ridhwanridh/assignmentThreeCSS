FERRERO-ROCHER

Exquisite Hazelnut Indulgence

A Delightful Blend of Texture and Flavor

Ferrero Rocher is a spherical chocolate sweet made by Italian manufacturer Ferrero SpA. It consists of a whole roasted hazelnut encased in a thin wafer shell filled with hazelnut chocolate and covered in milk chocolate and chopped hazelnuts.

1

Each Ferrero Rocher is wrapped in gold foil.

2

The name "Rocher" comes from the French word for rock or boulder.

3

The recipe has been a closely guarded secret for decades.
